About Me

Hola! I'm Tanvi Raut, an aspiring design student at the Indian School of Design and Innovation (ISDI). With a natural inclination towards design, I am deeply passionate about bringing art to life.

I thrive on creativity, constantly seeking to communicate complex ideas into simpler experiences. Beyond design, I'm an avid reader, a music enthusiast, and a nature lover who cherishes the beauty of all living creatures.

Welcome to my portfolio, where I merge my diverse interests and creative vision.

The photo series "Ominous," reveals the complex duality that exists inside people and offers a sad examination of human nature.

Every picture tells a story visually by showcasing the minute details in expression and that reveal possible hidden motives.
